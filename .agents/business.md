# Business & Monetization Strategy - Sol Spy

This document outlines the commercial strategy, growth loops, and monetization model for the **Sol Spy** Telegram Mini App.

## Monetization Model (The Money Maker)

### 1. Affiliate Referral Fees (Trojan Bot Integration)
- Every card renders a massive "⚡️ 1-Click Snipe" button.
- The button is a deep link pointing directly to Trojan Trading Bot (`https://t.me/solana_trojanbot?start=r-mahdi123-[token_address]`).
- Trojan Bot shares **20% to 35% of trading volume fees** generated by referred users back to the affiliate account.
- Since memecoins exhibit extremely high turnover rates and transaction frequencies, this affiliate pipeline offers a compounding recurring revenue stream.

### 2. Sponsored / Promoted Spots (Future Upgrade)
- Once active user count climbs, token developers can bid/pay to pin their token to the top of the Radar dashboard.
- This creates instant liquidity for new launches while generating direct advertising revenue.

## Viral Growth Loop

### "Locked Alpha" Loop
- Showing 3 highly active tokens build user trust and interest.
- Hiding the top 2 gems with highest momentum creates instant FOMO (Fear of Missing Out).
- The "Share to Unlock" button leverages native Telegram APIs to prompt sharing to groups/channels.
- This creates a viral cycle: users share screenshots or app links into trading groups, onboarding additional users at zero cost per acquisition (CAC).
